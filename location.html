Let's commence the description of my project

This project was carried out during your summer internship at BFI and aims to develop a car rental management system, allowing customers to reserve vehicles online and administrators to manage the car fleet and reservations


Project name: Take your car
Company: BFI
Technologies and tools used
Backend: J2EE (Java 2 Enterprise Edition)
Frontend: Angular

1. Main features

1.1. Main features

• Car catalog: Displaying vehicles available for rental with their details (model, make, year, price, etc.)

• Adding and Updating Vehicles: Features for administrators to add new vehicles and update existing information

• Vehicle health monitoring: Management of car availability and maintenance

1.2. Customer management

• Registration and authentication: Account creation and connection system for customers

• Customer Profile: Management of customers' personal information and rental preferences

1.3. Car reservation

• Search and Reservation:Customers can search for available cars according to their criteria and make reservations online

• Reservation management: Monitoring of current reservations, rental histories, and cancellations

1.4. Payment management

• Online payment: Integration of online payment solutions to allow customers to pay for their reservations

• Billing: Generating and sending invoices to customers after each reservation

1.5. Administrative dashboard

• Business overview: Dashboard for administrators displaying rental statistics, revenue generated, and fleet status

• User management: Features to manage customer and administrator accounts

2. Architecture

2.1. Backend (J2EE)

• API RESTful :Fournit des endpoints sécurisés pour les opérations de gestion des véhicules, des clients, et des réservations

• Gestion des données :Utilisation d'ORM (Object-Relational Mapping) pour interagir avec la base de données

• Security: Implementation of authentication and authorization mechanisms to protect sensitive data

2.2. Frontend (Angular)

• Responsive UI: Using Angular components to create a dynamic and responsive user interface

• Communication with the backend: Using Angular services to consume RESTful APIs provided by the J2EE backend

• User Experience: Intuitive and easy-to-use design for customers and administrators

3. Benefits for the company

• Operational efficiency: Automation of rental management processes, reducing time and manual effort

• Online Accessibility: Customers can book cars anytime and from anywhere

• Improved customer satisfaction: Intuitive user interface and simplified booking process

• Real-time tracking: Monitoring reservations and vehicle status in real time

• Error Reduction: Minimizing errors through automation and centralized data management



This project aims to modernize and optimize the car rental process at BFI, providing a better experience for customers and more efficient management for administrators
